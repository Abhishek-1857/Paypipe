Dodo Payments Integration

Dodo powers every
dollar that flows through

1

Checkout Session

We create a Dodo checkout with contractor + amount metadata via API.

2

Card Payment

Founder pays on Dodo's hosted checkout. Dodo handles compliance and processing.

3

Webhook Trigger

On payment.succeeded, Dodo fires a signed webhook to our backend.

4

USDC Settlement

We verify the signature, record the payout, and send USDC to the contractor's Solana wallet.

For scheduled payouts, metadata includes schedule IDs — the webhook also advances the recurring schedule automatically.
